About Us

A&V Distribution is a family-owned business dedicated to providing high-quality snacks to retailers across Toronto and the Greater Toronto Area. Founded by a passionate husband-and-wife team, we specialize in delivering premium products, starting with our flagship sunflower seeds. Sunflower seeds are a beloved snack deeply rooted in our cultural heritage, and we are proud to bring this iconic, flavourful treat to Canadian consumers.

Our mission is to offer reliable, seamless distribution services while building strong relationships with retailers and meeting the growing demand for flavourful, nutritious, and convenient snack options. As we expand, we aim to introduce a diverse range of products that cater to the evolving tastes of Canadian consumers, all while maintaining our commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and community.
